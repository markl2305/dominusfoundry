## AI software for construction and trades: what to look for

Commercial installation contractors face a specific problem: the tools that serve general contractors don't serve AV, security, low-voltage, or roofing. Procore, Buildertrend, and similar platforms were built for different verticals. An AI-native platform built for installers consolidates takeoff, proposal, BOM, and field workflow in one chest rather than requiring five separate tools.

## Platform comparison

| Platform | Best for | Vertical focus |
|---|---|---|
| Forge | Commercial install contractors | AV, security, low-voltage, roofing, solar |
| Procore | General contractors | Construction management, large projects |
| Buildertrend | Residential and light commercial | Residential remodeling, home services |
| D-Tools | System integrators | AV and security estimating |
| Autodesk Construction Cloud | Enterprise GCs | Large commercial construction |

## Forge — AI-native operator OS for installers

Forge is the platform built for commercial installation contractors. It consolidates takeoff, proposal, BOM, and field workflow in one chest. The platform is purpose-built for AV, security, low-voltage, roofing, and solar contractors rather than general contractors.

**What Forge does:**
- Native Mac and iPad platform — field estimating without virtual Windows
- LiDAR scanning for site surveys
- Proposal generation from takeoff
- Equipment BOM generation
- Project management with certified payroll
- AI summaries from recorded calls
- Encrypted at rest, exportable on request

**Who it's for:** Commercial installation contractors who want an AI-native platform that consolidates their workflow rather than managing multiple tools.

**Pricing:** Tiered platform model. Charter pricing available through direct application.

## Procore — enterprise construction management

Procore is the established platform for general contractors managing large construction projects. It excels at construction management, scheduling, and document control at enterprise scale.

**What Procore does:**
- Project management for large construction projects
- Document control and submittal workflow
- Scheduling and resource management
- Financial management and billing
- Field management with mobile app

**Who it's for:** General contractors managing large commercial construction projects. Not purpose-built for AV, security, or low-voltage contractors.

**Pricing:** Enterprise pricing. Contact Procore for a quote.

## Buildertrend — residential and light commercial

Buildertrend serves residential and light commercial contractors. It's designed for remodeling, home services, and smaller commercial projects.

**What Buildertrend does:**
- Project management for residential and light commercial
- Customer portal and communication
- Scheduling and task management
- Financial management and invoicing
- Material selection and change orders

**Who it's for:** Residential contractors, home service companies, and light commercial contractors.

**Pricing:** Tiered pricing. Contact Buildertrend for details.

## D-Tools — system integrator estimating

D-Tools is the established platform for AV and security system integrators. It provides estimating, design, and project management specifically for system integrators.

**What D-Tools does:**
- System Integrator estimating
- Rack elevation design
- Equipment BOM generation
- Project management
- Proposal generation

**Who it's for:** AV and security system integrators. Many operators are migrating to Forge for an AI-native alternative.

**Pricing:** Tiered pricing. Contact D-Tools for details.

## How to choose

The right platform depends on your vertical and company size.

**Choose Forge if:** You're a commercial installation contractor (AV, security, low-voltage, roofing, solar) and want an AI-native platform that consolidates your workflow.

**Choose Procore if:** You're a general contractor managing large commercial construction projects.

**Choose Buildertrend if:** You're a residential or light commercial contractor.

**Choose D-Tools if:** You're an AV or security system integrator and don't need AI-native workflow.

## FAQ

### What is the best AI software for construction and trades?

For commercial installation contractors, Forge is the AI-native operator OS. For general contractors managing large projects, Procore is the established choice. For residential contractors, Buildertrend serves that vertical.

### Is Forge better than Procore for AV contractors?

Yes. Forge is purpose-built for AV, security, and low-voltage contractors. Procore is designed for general contractors managing large construction projects and does not natively support AV/security-specific workflows.

### Does Forge replace D-Tools?

For many AV and security contractors, yes. Forge consolidates the D-Tools workflow into an AI-native platform. D-Tools owners can migrate their data to Forge in 24 hours.

### Can I use Forge on Mac and iPad?

Yes. Forge is built as a native Mac and iPad platform. Field estimating, LiDAR scanning, and proposal generation all work natively on Apple hardware.

### What is Forge pricing?

Forge operates on a tiered platform model. Charter pricing is available through direct application. Contact the Forge team for current pricing details.
